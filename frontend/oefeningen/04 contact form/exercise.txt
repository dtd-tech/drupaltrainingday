Use and customize the Drupal contact form.
Estimated time: 15 min.

==== Step 1 ====
Enable the contact form module.
 - To enable modules, go to the modules page at: Extend (/admin/modules).
 - Search for the modules 'Contact' and 'Contact block'. Install both modules.
 - To set permissions for the contact module, go to the permissions page at:
   People > Permissions (/admin/people/permissions).
 - Search for the Contact module and enable the permission:
   'Use the site-wide contact form' for 'Anonymous user' and 'Authenticated user'
 - Check the site-wide contact form at /feedback. Both logged-in and logged-out.
